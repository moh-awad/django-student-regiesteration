University Of Since And Technology

The University of Science and Technology is located in Omdurman, the largest city in Sudan and Khartoum State. It aims to expand the base of computer science computer, and applied science; starting with the faculties of information technology, computer science and computer engineering which became known as the electrical and electronic engineering faculties; with four departments (communication, medical engineering, electronic and computer engineering and computer programming).

Computer Science

Program Objectives:

This program aims at qualifying cadres in the field of computer science to contribute to meet the country’s need for qualified cadres in the science and computer applications that the country needs to fill the digital gap and build the economy and the information society.

The program also aims to graduate students who are qualified for self-learning, personal development, progress in high studies, and contribution to scientific research.
